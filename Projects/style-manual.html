Style Manual

A consistent approach to content for simpler and faster writing for government.

I was the full-time ongoing Interaction Designer APS6 with the Digital Transformation Agency (DTA) for the Australian Government Style Manual website project.


My key tasks involved producing the interaction design wireframes and prototypes using Sketch, Figma, Adobe XD (this was when UI design apps were most competitive - so I tried them all!) and code, conducting accessibility testing, user research analysis, moderating usability tests, information architecture (IA) tree testing, card sorting and facilitating design thinking workshops.


The Style Manual is used widely by the Australian government and is a key resource for roles such as content designers working in the APS.
