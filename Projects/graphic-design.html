iPad Mockups

I created these iPad mockups in Affinity Designer to be reusable and configurable. I wanted them for my new portfolio and didn’t want to pay for ones where I couldn’t change the presentation as easily. You can see I used them for my portfolio website. Each iPad orientation can be realigned on any orthographic projection and have the screen images and reflections changed easily to match the new angle.
