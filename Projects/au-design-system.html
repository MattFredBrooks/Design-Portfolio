Au Design System

Helping government create, test and release digital services quickly to people in need

I was the full-time ongoing Interaction Designer APS6 with the Digital Transformation Agency for the Australian Government Design System (AuDS) project.


My key tasks involved the interaction design and user research analysis running usability tests of the design system's website, writing design system guidance pages, collaborating on the design of user interface components and producing illustrations.


The Australian Government Design System was used by more than 100 Australian government websites and was a key resource for roles such as user interface designers and developers working in the APS. It was decommissioned in 2021. Due to its open and community supported design, it continues to be available as the GOLD Design System and used as an existing foundation for individual government departments.

Release Date

April, 2018
