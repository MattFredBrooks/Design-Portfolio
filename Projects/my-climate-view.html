From Prototype to Release: Unveiling the Design Process of an innovative product

From the 14 March 2022 for 14 months I was the User Interface Designer with the CSIRO in Data61 for the Climate Services for Agriculture (CSA) program's My Climate View project as a contractor. CSIRO's Data61 is the data and digital specialist arm of Australia's national science agency. I finished my role with the CSIRO on 16 June 2023.


My key tasks for the My Climate View project were to review the current prototype's design, usability and accessibility and make recommendations for improvements. 


As a result in part of my contributions to My Climate View, more than 11,200 users have trialled the tool since June 2021.
